How to Use

Hello, You! Welcome. Make yourself comfortable.

This is a simple Calorie Counter. Enter your estimated Calorie Budget in the Budget Section. Then, do you see the "Add Entry" button at buttom? Use that to add different entries of foods that you ate in breakfast, lunch, snacks, and dinner with their Calories. Also, add your Calorie Burns if you have workouted a bit. If you are lazy like me, then don't care about workout and leave that :3 Anyway, when you are ready, hit the Calculate button. If you want to destroy the record, hit Clear button.

Have fun!
